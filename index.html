Fighting for liberty

The Young Americans for Liberty at Washington University in St. Louis are the premiere source for liberty programming on campus. Our activism spans local, state and national politics, where we advocate strongly for the preservation of our personal and economic freedom. Our chapter, one of our hundreds across the country, is part of the largest network of pro-liberty college campus groups in the United States.

Be a part: join our group! We meet Mondays in DUC 232 where we dicuss liberty and plan our activism. Our recently launched chapter at Wash. U. is currently in the process of obtaining Student Union funding.
